What is PFOA?

Perfluorooctanoic acid (PFOA) is a synthetic chemical used in many industrial applications.

Health Risks

Linked to cancer, liver damage, and developmental issues. EPA recommends <2 ppt.

Safe Brands

Several brands show no detectable PFOA levels (ND) in recent testing.

PFOA Risk Levels

Low Risk

0 - 2 ppt

Medium Risk

2 - 10 ppt

High Risk

10 - 100 ppt

Very High Risk

100+ ppt
